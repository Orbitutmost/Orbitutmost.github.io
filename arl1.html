ARL Internship 2018

Primary Skills/Tasks

  • Learning new software
  • Research and open ended problems
  • CAD and design

Me, standing next to my presentation at the end of the summer

My first internship with ARL was through the Science and Engineering Apprentice Program. My goal for the summer was to retrofit a small UUV with new battery packs that would be safe and relatively inexpensive. To this end, with the help of a senior electrical engineer, I designed and prototyped a new battery pack with better form factor, life, and safety features. The original 3D printed shell and full battery pack can be seen in the images below. The oppertunity to work with the older engineers and learn from them was incredible, and I was a much better engineer by the end of it.
