UT Expects Near-normal Fall!


It was announced that UT, with the current progress of vaccinations, expects to have a near-normal fall semester on campus. This is great news for students, who's grades and social interactions have suffered greatly under quarentine. This announcement is dependant on students actually getting vaccinated, which UT says they cannot force. Some hybrid classes are still on the course schedule, presumable for students who will not be returning to campus.
