Hail in North Austin!


Residents in the suburbs north of Austin this week recevied the unwelcome surprise of a shock hailstorm this week. Within the hour, the sky went from clear to dark storm clouds, which drops hail as big as an inch in diameter. As fast as the storm appeared it was gone, but it left behind some vehicle damage.
