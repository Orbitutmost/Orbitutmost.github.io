UT Denmark Maymesters Approved!


This week, students in the UT Maymesters program were notified that as of April 21st, Denmark will be allowing study as a valid reason for travel. This is great news for students, who have been waiting in limbo. They could not plan internships or buy plane tickets until study was an allowed reason for entry in Denmark's quarentine, which is the update coming on the 21st.
