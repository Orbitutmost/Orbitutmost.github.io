New Avatar Series!


It was announced today that a new Avatar: the Last Airbender series will be coming to Youtube on the last day of Youtube's Earth Day celebration (April 24th, 2021). While no detials are known about the series yet, more will be released to us, along with a trailer, on April 19th.
