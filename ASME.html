During senior year, I served as the Technical Lead for the UT ASME student organization. My primary responsibility in this role was to ensure our members had access to opportunities to learn hands-on technical skills and experiment outside of their comfort zone. To that end, one of the programs I ran was a series of seminars covering a wide range of topics, including CAD, electronics / mechatronics, and python programming.
